💼 Job Board Platform

📖 Overview

The Job Board Platform is a web-based recruitment system that connects job seekers with employers through a centralized platform. It allows recruiters to post job openings, manage applications, and hire candidates efficiently, while job seekers can search, filter, and apply for jobs based on their skills, qualifications, and interests. The platform simplifies the hiring process and improves accessibility to employment opportunities.

🎯 Objectives

* Provide a centralized platform for job listings and recruitment.
* Enable job seekers to search and apply for jobs easily.
* Allow recruiters to post and manage job vacancies.
* Streamline the hiring and application process.
* Improve communication between employers and candidates.
* Reduce recruitment time and manual effort.


✨ Key Features

👤 User Authentication

* Secure registration and login.
* Separate roles for Job Seekers and Recruiters.

💼 Job Management

* Post, edit, and delete job vacancies.
* Manage job descriptions and requirements.

🔍 Advanced Job Search

* Search jobs by title, company, location, and skills.
* Apply filters based on salary, experience, and job type.

📄 Resume Upload

* Upload and manage resumes.
* Create professional candidate profiles.

📬 Application Tracking

* Apply for jobs online.
* Track application status in real time.

📊 Dashboard & Analytics

* Monitor active jobs and applications.
* View recruitment insights and statistics.
